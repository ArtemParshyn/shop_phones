Account & Billing

How do I manage my account online?

You can manage your Boost Mobile account completely online:

  1. Log in to your account dashboard
  2. Update personal information in Profile Settings
  3. View and pay bills in Billing section
  4. Change plans or services in My Plan
  5. Set up auto-pay for hassle-free billing

All account management is available 24/7 through our website.

What payment methods are accepted online?

We accept the following online payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Bank Transfer: Direct ACH payments from your checking account
  • Boost Credit: Apply for financing during checkout

All online payments are secured with 256-bit encryption.

Devices & Setup

How do I activate my device purchased online?

Activating your device is a simple online process:

  1. Unbox your device and ensure it's charged
  2. Log in to your Boost Mobile account
  3. Go to "Activate Device" section
  4. Enter your device's IMEI number (found in settings)
  5. Follow the step-by-step activation guide
  6. Restart your device when prompted

Most activations complete within 15 minutes.

How can I troubleshoot device issues remotely?

For common device issues:

  • Network Problems: Use our online network diagnostic tool
  • Software Issues: Access our step-by-step troubleshooting guides
  • Performance Problems: Run our device optimization tool in your account
  • Setup Assistance: Watch our video tutorials for device setup

If issues persist, submit a ticket through our support system.

Shipping & Returns

How do I return a device purchased online?

Our online return process:

  1. Log in to your account and go to Order History
  2. Select the item you wish to return
  3. Complete the return request form
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label
  5. Package the item securely and attach the label
  6. Drop off at any UPS location

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return.

What are your shipping options?

We offer multiple shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (Free on orders over $50)
  • Expedited Shipping: 2 business days ($9.99)
  • Next Day Shipping: Overnight delivery ($19.99)
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in select areas ($24.99)

All shipments include tracking and require signature upon delivery.

Ticket System

How does the ticket support system work?

Our ticket-based support system:

  • Submit: Create a ticket through your online account
  • Track: Monitor your ticket status in real-time
  • Communicate: Exchange messages with support agents
  • Resolve: Receive solutions within 24-48 hours
  • Feedback: Rate your support experience after resolution

All ticket history is stored in your account for future reference.

What information should I include when submitting a ticket?

For faster resolution, include:

  • Detailed description of your issue
  • Device model and software version
  • Account information (email/phone number)
  • Steps you've already tried to resolve the issue
  • Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)
  • Order number (for shipping/billing issues)

Complete information helps us resolve your issue faster.
